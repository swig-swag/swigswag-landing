Our History

Swig Swag millennial’s much-loved Indian eatery, first opened doors in the beautiful city of Ludhiana in Punjab. Swigwag is started by Ravi Roop in 2019.Swigswag is a representative of joyous and ecstatic vibes, and we strive every day to bring more of cheer in everyone’s life. Our core values and ethics have always driven us towards being a responsible brand and a establishment of value in the society Our swiggers are the most popular item on the Swigswag menu . During this joyful journey of the past 3 years, our hearts are filled with gratitude. In the coming years, with the same enthusiastic spirit, we look forward towards bringing these vibes across the country and spreading our wings of wonder to everyone with the best food, and the most wonderful Indian eatery experience.The goal has always revolved around delivering a beautiful experience and an aim for every customer to have a delicious conversation with food. As a team we are always on the go to deliver the best
